Borrow a Life is an MVP marketplace for borrowing a short, safe, public window into someone else's real life.

It is not a tour app, not a class marketplace, and not a mentor platform.
The goal is perspective.

People often make life decisions from the outside.
They imagine what a career, place, or lifestyle might feel like.
Borrow a Life turns that curiosity into a bounded real-world experience.

Users can explore windows like a Berlin software engineer, a vegetable farmer, a line cook, a street vendor, a startup founder, or someone starting over.

Each listing shows intent, duration, price, tags, and safety score.
The detail page explains the host, public itinerary, experience, and boundaries.

Before booking, the AI Safety Engine analyzes time, duration, location type, transport risk, public exit access, and itinerary.

If the request is safe, the app shows a safety score and generates a Boundary Contract:
what is allowed, what is not allowed, and what rules both sides must follow.

Borrow a Life is not about entering someone's private life.
It is about experiencing the public rhythm of their reality.

Most platforms help you book places.
Borrow a Life helps you borrow perspective.
